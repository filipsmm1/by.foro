Editorial policy

Last updated 19 July 2026

How by.foro selects, edits, labels and corrects editorial work.

Contact by.foro

Editorial independence

Stories are selected and edited according to their relevance to the by.foro point of view. Payment does not buy editorial coverage or a favourable conclusion.

Sources and accuracy

Factual claims should be checked against reliable sources. Opinion and aesthetic judgement are identified through context rather than presented as objective fact.

Commercial relationships

Sponsored work, gifted products and affiliate links are labelled clearly. Commercial participation does not remove the obligation to be accurate and useful.

Corrections

Material errors are corrected promptly. Meaningful changes to published stories are reflected in the updated date when appropriate. Corrections can be sent to hello@byforo.com.

Visual provenance

Original by.foro visual direction is identified as such. Third-party imagery should be licensed, credited or used with permission before publication.
