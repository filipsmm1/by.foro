Department notes

Three principles shaping this edit.

Short observations from the by.foro visual desk.

01

Mix eras, not themes

Rooms gain depth when objects arrive from different periods but share proportion or mood.

02

Use colour architecturally

The best colour decisions belong to walls, joinery and material, not scattered accents.

03

Leave evidence of life

A room becomes persuasive when it contains books, wear and the practical objects of a day.
