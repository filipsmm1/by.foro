Why Personal Style Matters Again

Fashion is moving beyond disposable trends and back toward identity, intention and individuality.

By by.foro · July 6, 2026 · 4 min read

Fashion

The algorithm made everyone fluent

People now recognise micro-trends almost instantly. That fluency is useful, but it also makes imitation easier and novelty shorter-lived.

Identity lasts longer than novelty

Personal style is not a fixed uniform. It is a system of preferences: proportions you return to, colours you trust, references that feel like yours and pieces you wear beyond their moment.

Buy for the repeat

The strongest wardrobe decision is often the least dramatic one: choosing an item because it improves what you already own and will still make sense after the feed moves on.

Taste becomes useful when it helps you choose, not only consume.
