Editorial policy

Effective July 6, 2026

Editorial mission

by.foro selects fashion, interiors, beauty, design and cultural coverage based on relevance, originality, visual intelligence and usefulness.

Human curation

Editorial selection is directed by people. Automated tools may assist with research, drafting, organisation or production, but final publication should be reviewed for accuracy, clarity, originality and fit.

Accuracy and sourcing

Factual claims should be checked against reliable sources. Reporting should distinguish fact from interpretation. Images, quotations and ideas should be attributed where required.

Beauty coverage

FORO Beauty focuses on aesthetics, campaigns, products and culture. Science-backed skincare and wellness claims should be handled through Stylish & Healthy or supported by appropriate primary and authoritative sources.

Commercial independence

Advertising, affiliate relationships, gifting and sponsorship do not automatically determine editorial coverage. Paid content should be labelled clearly, and affiliate links should be disclosed.

Conflicts of interest

Writers and editors should disclose material relationships that could reasonably affect coverage.

Corrections

Material errors should be corrected promptly and transparently. Correction requests can be sent through the contact page with the page URL, disputed statement and supporting evidence.

AI and synthetic media

Synthetic or materially altered imagery should not be presented as documentary reality. Where the context could mislead a reasonable reader, the use of AI-generated or significantly manipulated media should be disclosed.

Updates

This policy may evolve as by.foro expands its formats, contributors and commercial activity.

Important

This page is a practical launch template for a static editorial website. It should be reviewed against the final business entity, location, tools and data practices before public launch.
