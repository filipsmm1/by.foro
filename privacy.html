Privacy policy

Last updated 19 July 2026

How by.foro handles information submitted through the website.

Contact by.foro

Information you submit

When you use the contact or newsletter form, you provide details such as your name, email address, organisation, subject and message. Form submissions are processed by FormSubmit and forwarded to the by.foro email inbox.

Purpose and legal basis

Contact information is used to respond to enquiries, consider projects or pitches, handle corrections and maintain necessary business records. Newsletter requests are processed on the basis of consent.

Retention

Enquiries are kept only as long as reasonably necessary for communication, project administration, legal obligations or dispute resolution. Newsletter consent records are retained while the subscription is active and for a limited period afterwards where required.

Sharing

Personal data is not sold. It may be processed by hosting, email and form-delivery providers solely to operate the website and communications.

Cookies and analytics

The current site does not intentionally use advertising cookies or analytics scripts. The hosting provider may keep standard security and delivery logs.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or data portability. You may withdraw newsletter consent at any time by emailing hello@byforo.com.

Contact

Privacy requests can be sent to hello@byforo.com. Include enough detail to identify the relevant communication.
