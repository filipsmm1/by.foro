Privacy policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Scope

This policy explains how by.foro handles information when you visit this website or contact the brand.

Information you provide

The current contact form does not transmit data to a by.foro server. It prepares an email in your own email application. Information you send by email is processed only to respond to your message, manage a potential project, handle a correction or maintain necessary records.

Newsletter

The current newsletter field is a launch placeholder and does not store email addresses. Before a live mailing list is connected, this policy should be updated to identify the newsletter provider, legal basis, retention period and unsubscribe process.

Technical data

The static website itself does not intentionally set analytics or advertising cookies. The hosting provider may process standard server logs such as IP address, browser details, requested pages and timestamps for security and delivery.

Legal bases

Depending on the situation, personal data may be processed to respond to your request, take steps before entering a contract, perform a contract, comply with legal obligations or pursue legitimate interests such as site security and business administration.

Retention

Emails and related records are kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purpose they were collected, including legal, accounting and dispute-resolution needs.

Sharing

Personal data is not sold. It may be shared with service providers that support hosting, email, legal, accounting or other necessary operations, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to data portability. You may also complain to a competent data protection authority.

International processing

Some service providers may process data outside your country. Where required, appropriate transfer safeguards should be used.

Contact

Privacy questions and requests can be submitted through the contact page.

Important

This page is a practical launch template for a static editorial website. It should be reviewed against the final business entity, location, tools and data practices before public launch.
