What cookies are

Cookies are small text records stored by a website in your browser. Similar browser storage can serve related purposes. Some storage is necessary for a requested service; optional storage requires a valid choice where applicable.

How consent works

Until you make a choice, optional scripts marked for consent are blocked. “Accept optional” permits all optional categories currently configured; “Reject optional” keeps them blocked. Both actions save only the preference cookie listed above. The choice expires after 180 days, after which the site asks again.

Changing your choice

Select “Manage cookie preferences” on this page to reopen the controls. Saving a new choice replaces the old one immediately. You can also delete site data for byforo.com in your browser settings; the banner will then appear on your next visit.

Adding tools in future

Before analytics, advertising, embedded media or another optional service is enabled, this policy should be updated with its provider, purpose, cookie names and lifetimes. Its script must be classified in the consent system so it cannot load before acceptance.
