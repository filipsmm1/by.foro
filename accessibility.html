Accessibility

Updated July 6, 2026

Commitment

by.foro aims to make its website usable for a broad range of visitors, including people using keyboards, screen readers, zoom and other assistive technologies.

Current measures

The site uses semantic headings, labelled navigation, visible keyboard focus, descriptive alternative text, sufficient text sizing and responsive layouts.

Known limitations

Some decorative editorial artwork is abstract and may not communicate the same visual experience through alternative text. Third-party sites linked from by.foro are outside our control.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, send the page URL, a description of the issue and the browser or assistive technology used through the contact page.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility should be reviewed whenever new components, embeds, forms or third-party services are added.

Important

This page is a practical launch template for a static editorial website. It should be reviewed against the final business entity, location, tools and data practices before public launch.
