Affiliate links

Some future stories may contain affiliate links. If a reader purchases through one of those links, by.foro may receive a commission at no additional cost to the reader.

Editorial selection

Affiliate availability does not determine whether an item is included. A product or object should fit the editorial purpose before a commercial link is considered.

Sponsored work

Paid partnerships are labelled as sponsored or produced in partnership. Studio work for a brand is separate from independent editorial coverage.

Current status

At launch, pages without an affiliate label should be understood as non-affiliate editorial content.
